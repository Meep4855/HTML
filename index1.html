History Of 3D Printing.

During 1981 Hideo Kodama from the Nagoya Municipal Industrial research institute, had released his notes about away to quickly make prototype using photopolymerization (This is when a liquid, is used to make a model that a computer gives it. It is constructed by layers and harden by a ultraviolet light). In 1984 Charles Hull built upon Hideo Kodama notes and created something stereolithography(a form of 3d printing technology that is used for creating models, prototypes, patterns, and production parts by layer.) In 1992 Charles Hull was able to make a a machine that let people print complicated parts.There giving the birth to 3D printing.
